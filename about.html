    ABOUT MECHNIDO

    M/S MECHNIDO was Established in 2018 in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, M/S Mechnido is a promising enterprise in the electric vehicle (EV) industry. With a strong commitment to driving innovation and contributing to a sustainable future, Mechnido is dedicated to manufacturing high-quality electric vehicles that offer exceptional performance and efficiency. Our team of skilled engineers, designers, and visionaries works diligently to develop cutting-edge EVs that prioritize reliability and customer satisfaction.

    MISSION

    At Mechnido, our mission is to play a pivotal role in the advancement of the electric vehicle industry.

    We strive to manufacture electric vehicles that meet the highest standards of quality and performance while reducing the environmental impact. Our aim is to provide eco-friendly transportation solutions that contribute to a cleaner and greener future.

    VISION

    Mechnido envisions a future where electric vehicles become the mainstream choice for transportation.

    We aspire to be a key player in shaping this future by continually innovating and manufacturing electric vehicles that are not only efficient but also affordable and accessible. Through our dedication and efforts, we aim to contribute to a sustainable and healthier planet.
